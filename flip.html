Rules

  • The game is played on a 6x8 grid that incorporates 48 squares.
  • Players must select their mark before the game starts (either X or O) and must play the entire game using the same mark.
  • After turn of each player the entire row gets reversed with 50% chance. Other rules stay same.
  • The first player to align 4 of their identical symbols (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game
